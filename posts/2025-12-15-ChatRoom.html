Just released v1.1.0

I have been working on several web development projects and this is one of them.

It's a public chat app with very few restrictions. It accepts maximum 50 concurrent users, you cannot spam more than 100 messages per minute and there cannot be multiple concurrent users with the same nickname.

It's open source and I don't intend any profit on it, but I really appreciate some feedback. Feel free to fork if you'd like.
